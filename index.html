Hawam App – Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last updated: May 25, 2025

Privacy Policy

Hawam is a private business management application designed exclusively for managers and workers. It is not intended for public distribution or general use.

Information We Collect

  • Customer names, phone numbers, and addresses
  • Appointment details and related notes
  • Employee names, attendance, and work schedules
  • Project records linked to specific customers

All information is securely stored using Google Firebase Firestore.

Permissions Used

  • Notifications: To alert managers of upcoming appointments
  • Internet Access: Required for real-time synchronization with Firebase
  • Manual Sharing: Admins may manually share project or customer information via WhatsApp or Gmail for coordination purposes

Backend Services

  • Firebase Authentication: Used to verify and restrict user access
  • Role-Based Access Control: Ensures that only authorized managers can perform administrative actions
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: Automate appointment reminders and background processing
  • Push Notifications: Sent to managers 3 hours before scheduled appointments
  • SMS Reminders: Customers receive reminders 24 hours prior via Vonage API

All communications are secure and limited strictly to operational purposes.

Data Usage & Sharing

Data collected by the Hawam app is used solely for managing business operations including appointments, employees, and customer records. No data is shared or sold to third parties. Any sharing through email or messaging is performed manually by the admin.

Security

User access is restricted through Firebase Authentication. Critical actions are protected by role-based permissions to ensure proper data security.
